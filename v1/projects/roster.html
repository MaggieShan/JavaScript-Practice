At the beginning of each new semester or school year, teachers are faced with the challenge of remembering names for a large number of new students. To solve this problem, I designed an experience to help educators match faces to names, with the goal of shortening the time needed to reach complete un-aided accuracy.

Users have the option of connecting to google classroom, adding classes, students and their interest, and remembering them through a cue-card-like game.

My Roles: Designed graphics through Adobe Illustrator and created interface functionality through Invision.
